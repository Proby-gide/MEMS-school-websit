About Us

Learn more about our school's history, vision, and mission

Our School

Shree Malatesh English Medium School, located in Gourapur, TQ DI: Haveri 581110, is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students from LKG to 10th Standard.

Our school has been serving the community with excellence in education, focusing on both academic achievement and character development. We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for future challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and a commitment to social responsibility. To create a community of learners where every student feels valued, respected, and inspired to reach their full potential. To prepare students to be successful in a rapidly changing world, contributing to a more just and sustainable future. To foster a culture of curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong pursuit of knowledge.

Our Mission

To provide a challenging and supportive environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. To empower students to become confident, creative, and compassionate global citizens. To cultivate critical thinking, collaboration, and a passion for learning in every student.

Founder's Message

Founder: Sri Basavaraj L Totager
President of Malatesh Education Society
Founding Year: 2010

The Shree Malatesh English Medium School was founded back in the year 2010 with a clear idea to provide quality and holistic education to our future generation. This school was established with the great motive to nurturing the most skill-full and creative minds of the society and we have been working in the same direction for years.

We provide them the knowledge pertaining to every subjects/fields in their life. Our curriculum is designed in such a way that it helps students develop comprehensive understanding and practical skills.

At our school we strive to equip our children with qualities and moral that are needed to face upcoming challenges in future life. We train students to be self confident, courageous and helpful in all scenarios. Developing humane behaviour and leadership qualities and imparting career oriented education is considered as most priority.

We know the significance of the homely school environment. So we maintain healthy home school relationship for the better development of the students. We encourage teachers and parents to interact with each other about the behaviour, performance and achievements of the students.

We ourselves feel blessed for working towards the noble deed of education. I feel pride and honour when I look back and observe what we served to the society and this is combined effort of us and the teachers and parents.

Principal's Message

A community dedicated to fostering academic excellence, creativity and character development. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that empowers students to reach their full potential and become responsible global citizens.

At Shree Malatesh English Medium School, we prioritize:

  • Academic Rigor: Challenging curriculum and innovative teaching methods
  • Holistic Development: Encouraging extracurricular activities, sports and arts
  • Values Based Education: Emphasizing empathy, respect and social responsibilities

Our school provides a safe, nurturing environment where students can explore their interests, develop their skills, and build lasting friendships. We are transitioning from Karnataka State Board to CBSE curriculum to provide our students with better opportunities for higher education.

Together, we can achieve great things. I invite you to join our school family and be part of our educational journey.
