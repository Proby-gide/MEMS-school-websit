Our curriculum and educational approach

Curriculum

We currently follow the Karnataka State Board curriculum and are transitioning to CBSE to provide our students with better opportunities for higher education and competitive examinations.

Current: Karnataka State Board

Comprehensive curriculum focusing on regional language, culture, and state-specific educational standards.

Transitioning to CBSE

Enhanced curriculum with national standards, better preparation for competitive exams, and wider college acceptance.
