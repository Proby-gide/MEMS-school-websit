Admissions

Join our school family - Admissions are now open!

Admission Process

1

Application

Fill out the admission form with all required details and submit it along with necessary documents.

2

Entrance Test

Students may be required to take an entrance test based on their class level and previous academic performance.

3

Interview

Personal interview with the student and parents to assess compatibility with our school values and academic standards.

4

Document Verification

Verification of all submitted documents including previous academic records, birth certificate, and other required certificates.

5

Admission Confirmation

Upon successful completion of all steps, admission will be confirmed with fee payment and enrollment completion.

Required Documents

For All Students

  • Birth Certificate (Original + 2 copies)
  • Aadhar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Passport Size Photographs (6 copies)
  • Address Proof
  • Parent ID Proof
  • Income Certificate

For Transfer Students

  • Transfer Certificate
  • Previous Academic Records
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Medical Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Medium of Instruction Certificate
